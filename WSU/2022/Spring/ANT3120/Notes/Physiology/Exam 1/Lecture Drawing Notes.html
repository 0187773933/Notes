Antidiuretic Hormone ( ADH )

  • Increase in ADH release

    • Increase in H2O transport into the blood

      • Decreases blood osmolarity

  • Decrease in ADH release

    • Decrease in H2O transport into the blood

      • Increase in blood osmolarity

  1. Dehydration or eat a lot of food
  2. Blood osmolarity increases
  3. Increases ADH release
  4. H2O is transported into the blood
  5. Blood osmolarity decreases
  1. Over-hydration or starvation
  2. Blood osmolarity decreases
  3. Decrease in ADH release
  4. Decreases H2O transport into the blood
ADH Release ∝ H2O transported into the blood
