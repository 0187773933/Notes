• SV = Stroke Volume

    • Volume of blood ejected to each great artery = ~70 mL

  • EDV = End Diastolic Volume

    • Volume of blood in ventricles prior to ejection of blood = ~120 mL

  • EF = Ejection Fraction

    • Percentage of blood ejected from each ventricle = 55% to 70%
EF=SVEDV∗100%

  • ESV = End Systolic Volume

    • Volume of blood in the ventricles after the stroke volume is ejected
ESV=EDV−SVSV=EDV−ESVEDV=SV+ESV

  • HR = Heart Rate = beats / minute

  • CO = Cardiac Output

    • volume of blood pumped by either the left or right ventricle per minute = ( 70 mL / min ) * ( 70 beats / min )
CO=SV∗HR
