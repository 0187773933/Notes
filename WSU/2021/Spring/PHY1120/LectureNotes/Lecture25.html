Driven Oscillators

  • Add a "motor" or "driver" - something that forces oscillations at a particular frequency

  • Think pumping legs on swing set

    • If you pump "too fast" , you don't swing very high
    • If you pump "too slow" , you don't swing very high
    • If you pump "just right" , you have more fun ; big oscillations

  • This is called "resonance"

  • Occurs when driving force ( pumping legs ) is at the correct frequency
