Pre-Quiz

  1. Which of the following is a correct statement of the 2nd law of thermodynamics?

A system is likely to spontaneously adopt the macrostate with the greatest number of microstates.

  1. Why doesn't a chair spontaneously start to move across the floor?

Because it is highly unlikely that the molecules of the floor would all get organized and push the chair in a single direction

 

2nd Law of Thermodynamics

Coin Flips:

Microstate = H T T H H T H

Macrostate = 4 Heads, 3 Tails

  • Each macrostate has number of arrangements ( microstates ) that correspond to the macrostate

  • So arrangements can be:

    • A specific sequence of coin flips
    • The locations of every molecule
    • The energies of every molecule

  • One statement of 2nd law:

If the number of particles is large, a system will spontaneously move toward the macrostate with the greatest number of microstates
