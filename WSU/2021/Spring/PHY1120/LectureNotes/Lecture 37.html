  • Waves exist in 2-D and 3-D , "ripples in a pond"

  • Energy gets "spread out" in 2-D and 3-D cases - ripples die down with distance

  • 2 Slit Explanation

    • Each slit is a "point source" of an expanding wave - the crests going out
    • When crest from top slit coincides ( at some location ) with crest from other slit , you will get the maximum signal ( constructive interference )
    • When crest from 1 slit coincides with valley from other , you will get zero signal ( destructive interference )

  • Math of Constructive and Destructive Interference :

Δϕ=2∗π∗Δrλ
Δr=|r1−r2|

  • Where r1 is the distance from slit 1 to the point of interest

    • A point on the screen

  • Where r2 is distance from slit 2 to a point on the screen

  • If Δϕ is an even multiple of π , (0,2π,4π,etc) , then constructive interference occurs

    • This is the same thing as saying that :

      • Δr = an integer multiple of λ:Δr=0,λ,2λ,etc

  • If Δϕ is an odd multiple of π , (1π,3π,5π,etc) , then destructive interference occurs

    • This is the same thing as saying that :

      • Δr = a half integer multiple of λ:0.5λ,1.5λ,2.5λ,etc
