Pre-Quiz

  1. In an electromagnetic wave traveling horizontally, the location of the electric field moves up and down vertically in space

False

The magnitude of the electric field increases and decreases, but it doesn't make sense to discuss "moving" the electric field since it isn't an object.

  1. If an electromagnetic wave is traveling in the x direction, the electric field must be pointing in the y direction.

False

The electric field could point in the z direction, or it could be changing its direction in the y-z plane as time goes on.
