Hanging Mass on Spring

  • Motion is the same as for horizontal mass attached to spring without friction
  • The confusion is "what is equilibrium length?"

  • The mass m stretches spring by xS
  • How much stretch? Use F=−k∗x
  • At new equilibrium:

  • Forces balance so: mass∗gravity=k∗xS
  • ∴xS=m∗gk
  • But main point, all oscillation occurs about new equilibirum
  • No forces are exerted on mass at new equilibrium
  • If we choose coordinate system:

  • Motion = y(t)=A∗cos((ω0∗t)−ψ)
