Electric Potential

  • For a single charge, V=kC∗qr

  • V is very large and positive when near a positive charge

  • V is very small ( still positive ) when far from a positive charge

  • V is very large and negative when near a negative charge

  • V is very small ( still negative ) when far from a negative charge

  • Notice, there are no vectors in this equation.

    • There is no "direction" to electric potential

  • If the system has multiple charges, find total V at a point in space by adding up values of V due to each charge
