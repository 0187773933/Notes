Wave Model of Light

  • What is moving?

    • For strings , it's the bits of mass on string :

    • For Sound , it's the air molecule :

    • For light ? It's the electric and magnetic field amplitude :

      • As time goes on , the "max up" location travels to the right

  • So light is this electric field wave ( and magnetic field wave at the same time )

  • Is there any matter involved? Is any physical thing with matter oscillating?

    • No. No matter needed. EM waves can travel through vacuum.

      • Thats how we see stars

    • But if the EM wave comes across a charged particle, the electric field in the wave will make the charged particle oscillate up and down

  • Where is the photon?

    • It can't be visualized easily in this view. It takes quantum mechanics, and more wave math to describe it

  • Still use wave equations though

    • E=E0∗sin((k∗x)−(ω∗t))
    • E is the electric field amplitude
    • E0 is the maximum electric field amplitude

  • In vacuum

    • Wave velocity = v=c=ωk=λ∗f

    • c is the speed of light = 3∗108 meters1 second
